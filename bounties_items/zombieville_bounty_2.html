ZOMBIEVILLE LONGPLAY BOUNTY

As said before, ZombieVille [1997] is a DOS game that was released by Psygnosis in an uncompleted state. It is considered abandon-ware as the game received negative initial reception and forgotten through the years.
There's little to no coverage online of this MS-DOS game as of today, even with videos! The following video is a YouTube playlist that showcases game-play of ZombieVille 1997...in 2020!
More than 20 years since its release date!

As seen in the embedded Youtube playlist of the game, one can see that the game-play is not entirely complete. It only reaches up to a certain point because ZombieVille was designed to zombify Matt Black to the point to force a GAME OVER if Matt Black was ever hit by a zombie.
However, it's not made clear if zombies have the power to zombify by meleeing you AND WITH THEIR BULLETS. Not only that but the reason why the videos were split into multiple parts was because ZombieVille has the tendency to CRASH TO DESKTOP at random times.
However, one guaranteed method to crash the game is to shoot down certain NPC's at certain times. For certain NPC's, the game will crash outright. However for some NPC's, the game will spawn zombies around Matt Black for the intent of killing Matt fast! If you kill these zombies before they kill you, the game will immediately crash to desktop, as it seems that the game wasn't programmed to handle this exception.
I believe that the reason for this exception not programmed in was either of the following:

  • Time constraints may have not allowed this situation exception to be taken account of.
  • Zombies emmit a lot of blood particles upon death (to hide the design choice of body sprites disappearing on the fly. Having too many blood particles will probably slow down the game to a crawl.
  • Or time constraints may have not allowed to have the engine handle a large amount of zombies on screen at any scene.

Also, I do believe that zombies have the rare chance of dropping antidotes since they do drop medkits, but I haven't seen this happen in 2020.
